Book Conditions Guidelines

Like New

1. An unread copy with cover and original plastic protective wrapping intact.

2. All in good condition

3. Doesn't have any damages to it's cover, pages and it's outer layer

Used - Very Good

1. All pages and cover are intact.

2. Books may have minor cosmetic defects, e.g. marks, wears, cuts, bends, and crushes on the cover, spine, pages, or dust cover if applicable.

3. Dust cover is intact and pages are clean without notes.

4. Books may contain remainder marks on outside edges.

Used - Good

1. All pages and cover are intact.

2. Spine and cover may show signs of wear and minor foxing.

3. Pages may include limited notes and highlighting.

4. May include "From the library of labels.

5. Dust cover may be missing.

6. Books may contain remainder marks on outside edges.

Used - Acceptable

1. All pages and cover are intact.

2. Spine and cover may show signs of wear and foxing.

3. Pages may include limited notes, highlighting, or minor water damage but the text is readable.

4. May include "From the library of" labels.

5. Dust cover may be missing.

6. Books may contain remainder marks on outside edges.

Used - Poor

1. Pages and cover may not be intact.

2. Spine and cover may show signs of heavy wear and foxing.

3. Pages may include notes, highlighting, or water damage but the text is readable.

4. May include "From the library of" labels.

5. Dust cover may be missing.

6. Books may contain remainder marks on outside edges.
